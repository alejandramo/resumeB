Resume

She studied industrial design at the National Autonomous University of Mexico. In 2012 was part of the Mayan Design Winter Workshop work team, later she collaborated in museography projects for the Indigenous Museum of the National Commission for the Development of Indigenous Peoples. In 2014 she joined the project team "Binomios Creativos" of the Design Center of Oaxaca. In addition, she has worked with small producers and in some NGO's that use design as a tool for transformation. She is interested in collaborative design, sustainable and social.
